  • On June 26, 2022, Challenge Moscow 2022 will take place, one of the most interesting races of the international Challenge series.

    Оn the wide roads of the Sadovoe Koltso, past the Kremlin towers, triathletes will be able to show the highest speeds and set new world records for taking the Middle Distance.

    CHALLENGE MOSCOW is the first Middle Distance race in Russia. This, together with the exciting side events taking place the day after the main event, will target all triathletes, artists, celebrities, entrepreneurs and sports fans.

    CHALLENGE MOSCOW will be the centre of activity for both the Middle Distance as well as the unique side events, which include:

    • Middle Distance Triathlon

    • Sprint Distance Triathlon with celebrities and Russian top businessman’s

    • 5 km Man’s & Women’s run

    • 1km Junior

    • In the most beautiful park in Moscow, Park Novodevichy Ponds, tents for athletes and recreation areas will be set up, a wonderful evening program with fireworks

    What do you get from participating in Challenge Moscow 2022?

    1. Sportmaster bonuses for registration to the event (all distances). The cost of the slots will be returned to your Sportmaster card as bonuses – 5000 bonus. You can spend these bonuses to shopping in any Sportmaster shop.

    2. Challenge Moscow bonuses with Challenge points by downloading the ChallengeDrive mobile application from http://challengedrive.ru. The full amount for a slot in the Challenge points is in your app. You can spend your points at the event and Challenge partners.

    3. When registering a group of three people you get a discount of 10% of your slot cost. To get your discount please write an email to sport@challengemoscow.ru.

    4. VIP area for athletes and guests (recovery massage zone, non-alcoholic beer, cold champagne, music concert, best DJs, +10 friends in the VIP area for each participant).

    5. Privileges for corporate teams (our media will mention your company, your flags will be placed in our EXPO zone, your speakers will be presented on our forum).
